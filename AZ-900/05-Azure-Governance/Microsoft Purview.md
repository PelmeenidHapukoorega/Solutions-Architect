## My interpretations

## Microsoft Purview

### Why does it exist?

To provide a single, unified view for managing data governance, risk and compliance across on prem, multicloud, and SaaS environments.

### What does it do?

It enables automated data discovery, identifies sensitive information and tracks end to end data lineage to understand the datas origin and movement.

### How does it work?

It integrates two main areas: Risk and Compliance and unified data governance to scan and classify your entire data landscape automatically.

### Summary

Microsoft Purview is like a master catalog and map for all your companys data in other words it helps you find where everything is stored, see whats private and make sure you are following safety rules.

With it you can stay up to date on your data landscape thanks to:

* Automated data discovery.
* Sensitive data classification.
* E2E data lineage aka end to end.

## Risk and compliance solutions

MS 365 features are a core component of Purview risk and compliance solutions. Teams, Onedrive and Exchange are just some of the services of it that Purview uses to help manage and monitor your data. 

By managing and monitoring your data Purview is able to help organize the following:

* Protect senisitive data across clouds, apps and devices.
* Identifiy data risks and manage regulatory complicane and requirements.
* Get started with regulatory complicance.

## Unified data governance

### Why does it exist?

To solve the problem of managing data scattered across different locations (on prem, multiple clouds and databases) from a single spot.

### What does it do?

It provides tools to manage and govern data stored in Azure, SQL, Hive, local servers and even competing clouds like Amazon S3.

### How does it work?

It connects to diverse data sources and brings them into a central management system to apply consistent rules and oversight.

### Summary

It acts as a central control center that lets you oversee all your companys data no matter if its on your own computer, in Azure or even in AWS cloud.

Purviews unified data governance helps your organization:

* Create an up to date map of your entire data estate which inlcudes data classification and E2E lineage.
* Identify where sensitive data is stored in your estate.
* Create a secure environment for data consumers to find valuable data.
* Generate insights about how your data is stored and used.
* Manage access to the data in your estate securely and at scale.
