# Describe the purpose of Azure Advisor

## My interpretations

## Azure Advisor

Evaluates your resources and makes recommendations to help improve reliability, security and performance, achieving operational excellence and reducing costs. Its designed to helo you save time on cloud optimization.

### Why does it exist?

To act as a personalized cloud consultant that helps you follow best practices and optimize your resources automatically.

### What does it do?

It scans your environment and provides actionable recommendations across five key pillars: Cost, Security, Reliability, Performance, and Operational Excellence.

### How does it work?

It analyzes your usage telemetry and configuration, then presents Quick Fixes in a dashboard, sends alerts for new tips, and allows you to snooze or dismiss suggestions.

5 Categories of recommendation:

* Reliability: is used to ensure and improve the continuity of your business-critical applications.
* Security: is used to detect threats and vulnerabilities that might lead to security breaches.
* Performance: is used to improve the speed of your applications.
* Operational Excellence: is used to help you achieve process and workflow efficiency, resource manageability and deployment best practices.
* Cost: is used to optimize and reduce your overall Azure spending.
